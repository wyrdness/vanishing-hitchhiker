## Recommended Reading Order

For those new to the subject:

1. **Start here**: Brunvand (1981) - *The Vanishing Hitchhiker* - Essential reading
2. Beardsley & Hankey (1942) - Historical academic perspective
3. Guiley (2007) - Broader context of ghost folklore
4. Dégh (2001) - Theoretical framework for understanding legends
5. Regional studies - For specific examples and local variants
